# NexusFlow Product Vision 2026

**Key Initiatives**:

- Custom report builder

## Product Principles

### 1. Simple by Default, Powerful on Demand

Start simple. Don't overwhelm users with complexity upfront. Let power users discover advanced features when they need them.

### 2. AI as Copilot, Not Replacement

AI should augment human decisions, not make them. Keep users in control while making them more effective.

### 3. Every Click Has a Purpose

Eliminate unnecessary steps. If users need to click more than 3 times for common actions, we've failed.

### 4. Data is Sacred

Users trust us with their data. Protect it fiercely. Be transparent about how it's used.

### 5. Build for Scale

What works for 10 users should work for 10,000. Design systems, not one-offs.
