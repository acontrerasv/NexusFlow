## Key Insights

### Positive Trends

1. **DAU growing steadily** - 10.7% growth QoQ
2. **Workflow volume up** - 22% more runs
3. **AI Assist adoption** - 4pp increase despite low base
4. **Success rate improving** - 94.2% workflow success

### Areas of Concern

1. **Stickiness (DAU/MAU)** - 26% vs 35% target
2. **Activation rate declining** - 45% vs 65% target
3. **TTFV increasing** - 12 days vs 3 day target
4. **CRM Sync adoption** - Only 34% connected

### Opportunities

1. **AI Assist** - Low adoption but high retention impact
2. **Templates** - Good adoption, can drive activation
3. **Mobile/PWA** - Only 8% usage, unmet need
4. **Scheduled workflows** - Low awareness, high value

---

## Recommendations

### Immediate

1. **Fix onboarding drop-off** - 22% drop before starting
2. **Simplify CRM connection** - 26% drop at this step
3. **Promote AI Assist** - Correlates with retention

### Q2 Priorities

1. **Reduce TTFV to <5 days** - New onboarding flow
2. **Increase CRM adoption to 50%** - CRM Sync v2
3. **Improve stickiness to 30%** - Engagement features
