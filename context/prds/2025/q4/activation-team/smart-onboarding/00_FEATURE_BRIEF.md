## Problem Statement

New users take an average of 12 days to reach first value (TTFV), resulting in 45% activation rate. Research shows that users who don't activate within 7 days have 3x higher churn.

## Solution

An AI-guided onboarding experience that personalizes the setup flow based on user role, industry, and goals, reducing TTFV from 12 days to 3 days.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Before | After | Status |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| TTFV | 12 days | 3 days | ✅ 4.2 days |
| Activation Rate | 45% | 65% | ⚠️ 52% |
| Onboarding Completion | 34% | 70% | ✅ 68% |

## Key Features

1. **Role-Based Paths** - Different flows for Sales Reps, Managers, and Ops
2. **AI Goal Setting** - AI suggests relevant workflows based on stated goals
3. **Progress Tracking** - Visual progress indicator with time estimates
4. **Skip Options** - Allow users to skip steps and return later
5. **Sample Data Mode** - Explore with pre-populated sample data

## Dependencies

- CRM OAuth improvements
- Analytics tracking
