# NexusFlow Business Context

> Complete business overview for the NexusFlow product team.

## Company Overview

### About NexusFlow

**NexusFlow** is an AI-powered sales automation platform designed for B2B teams in EMEA. Founded in 2023 and headquartered in Amsterdam, we help sales teams automate their workflows, integrate with existing CRMs, and leverage AI to optimize their sales processes.

### Mission
> Empower every sales team to work smarter through intelligent automation.

### Vision
> Become the leading sales automation platform for B2B teams in Europe by 2028.

### Core Values
1. **Customer Obsession** - Every decision starts with the customer
2. **Intelligent Simplicity** - Powerful features, simple experience
3. **Trust & Transparency** - Earn trust through honest communication
4. **Continuous Innovation** - Always improving, always learning

## Strategic Priorities

### Q2 2026 Focus Areas

#### 1. Enterprise Churn Reduction (P0)
**Problem**: Enterprise churn at 12.3% is unsustainable and threatens Series B.

**Root Causes**:
- Integration complexity (28%)
- Feature gaps (25%)
- Support issues (22%)
- Adoption challenges (19%)

**Key Initiatives**:
- CRM Sync v2 (improved integration)
- Enterprise support expansion
- Usage-based health scoring

#### 2. Enterprise Growth (P1)
**Goal**: Double enterprise customers from 156 to 312.

**Approach**:
- Enterprise-specific features (SAML, audit logs)
- Dedicated enterprise sales motion
- Customer success investment

#### 3. Activation Improvement (P2)
**Problem**: Only 45% of users reach activation (first workflow run).

**Goal**: Increase to 65% activation rate.

**Initiatives**:
- Smart onboarding redesign
- AI-guided setup
- Template recommendations

#### 4. NRR Expansion (P3)
**Goal**: Increase NRR from 108% to 115%.

**Levers**:
- Usage-based pricing tier
- Add-on modules
- Tier upgrades
