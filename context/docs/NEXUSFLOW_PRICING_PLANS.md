## FAQ

**Can I try before buying?**
Yes! 14-day free trial on Professional plan, no credit card required.

**What happens if I exceed limits?**
You'll receive warnings at 80% and 100%. Overages are billed monthly.

**Can I change plans mid-cycle?**
Upgrades are immediate and prorated. Downgrades take effect next cycle.

**Do you offer refunds?**
Monthly plans: No refunds. Annual plans: Pro-rated refund within 30 days.

**Is there a minimum commitment?**
Monthly plans have no commitment. Annual plans are 12-month commitment.
